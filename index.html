Bid to Win, inspired by "The Price Is Right"

Bid to Win: an online browser game where you have to guess the price of each shown item. Getting the answer correct will earn you 100 points. You have to reach a total score of 700 points to win, and you only have 10 items to guess from, game, good luck!
