(title) an online browser game inspired by "The Price Is Right"

NO REFUND! is a online browser game where you have to guess the price of each shown item. Getting the answer correct will earn you 100 points. You have to reach a total score of 1,000 points to win, and you only have 20 items to guess from. You have to earn the 1,000 points before or on the 20th-item question. or you lose the game, good luck!
